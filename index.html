Why you should enable your Geolocation

To experience all the benefits of the Steady Eats Application please enable your geolocation data by clicking the 'Enable Geolocation' button and allowing your location to be shared. This App uses your current location to find nearby restaurants and stores. Your location is what determines the center of the map, and the origin point for driving location, distance, and duration.
